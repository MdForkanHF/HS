Why the Little Things in a Relationship Mean Everything

Grand gestures of love can bring surprise and joy to your partner, but the little things in relationships may consistently strengthen your bond and increase emotional security.

Would you prefer having your partner gift you a new car or show you one small act of affection and thoughtfulness daily? For many people, daily affirmations and acts of love and caring mean more than a singular, grand gesture. The little things in relationships can be constant reminders that your partner thinks of you and cares about you. Small acts count because they can help: reduce uncertainty provide a sense of emotional security solidify the bond between two people increase empathy reduce the chance of growing apart

In subtle ways, the little things, more than the grand gestures, can strengthen your relationship and make love last.
